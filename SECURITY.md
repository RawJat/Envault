## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of Envault seriously. If you have discovered a security vulnerability, please do NOT create a public issue.

### Immediate Contact

Please report the vulnerability by emailing [dinanathdash@example.com](mailto:dinanathdash@example.com). Please include:

- A description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Potential impact.

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours and will provide a timeline for addressing the issue.

### Disclosure Policy

- We ask that you do not disclose the vulnerability to the public until we have had a chance to fix it.
- We will notify you when the fix is released.
